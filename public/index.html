How AutoFlow works

1

Customer arrives or books

Walk-in or QR code self check-in. Service advisor captures vehicle details, complaint, and damage photos in the digital intake form.

2

Diagnose & estimate

Technician diagnoses the issue and creates a detailed estimate with parts and labour. Customer approves via WhatsApp link.

3

Repair & track in real-time

Job moves through the pipeline. Technician clocks in/out. Manager sees live floor status. Customer gets automatic status updates.

4

Complete & delight

Before/after photos sent to customer. Invoice generated. Cashier processes payment. Customer receives digital service booklet.
