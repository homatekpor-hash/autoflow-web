Simple process

How AutoFlow
works

01

Customer arrives

Walk-in or QR self check-in. Advisor captures vehicle, complaint, and damage photos in the digital intake form.

02

Diagnose & estimate

Technician diagnoses and creates a detailed estimate. Customer approves via WhatsApp link.

03

Repair & track live

Job moves through the pipeline. Technician clocks in/out. Manager sees live floor status. Customer gets updates.

04

Complete & delight

Before/after photos sent to customer. Invoice generated. Customer receives digital service booklet.
